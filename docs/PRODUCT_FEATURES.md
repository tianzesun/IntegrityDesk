# Course Pulse Product Features: Analytics from Waiting List Data

## Overview

Course Pulse transforms daily university waiting list data into actionable insights for capacity planning, resource allocation, and student success optimization. This document details the complete feature set enabled by processing waiting list files.

## 🚀 **Core Analytics Capabilities**

### Data Processing Engine

**✅ Real-time Data Import**
- Processes TSV waiting list files daily
- Parses student demographics, course enrollment data, timestamps
- Maintains student course preferences and enrollment history
- Updates operational metrics every 24 hours

**✅ Student Academic Profiling**
- Identifies enrollment patterns by academic year and department
- Tracks course appeal and demand velocity
- Maps student preferences across academic disciplines

### Capacity Planning Intelligence

**✅ Seat Deficit Calculator**
- Calculates total section/sections needed across all courses
- Provides course-specific capacity recommendations
- Considers tutorial capacity limits vs enrollment demand
- Recommends additional sections, lecture halls, or faculty allocation

**✅ Demand Forecasting**
- Analyzes enrollment velocity (new signups per day)
- Predicts seasonal enrollment patterns
- Identifies courses with sudden demand spikes
- Forecasts capacity needs for upcoming terms

**✅ Course Health Monitoring**
- **Red Alerts**: Average wait >30 days (urgent intervention needed)
- **Yellow Alerts**: Average wait 15-29 days (attention needed)
- **Green Status**: Average wait <15 days (optimal capacity)

### Student Success Analytics

**✅ Veteran Student Identification**
- Locates students waiting >30 days across ANY course
- Prioritizes long-term waiting students for accommodation planning
- Tracks chronic wait list participation patterns
- Supports equity assessment and student support allocation

**✅ At-Risk Student Detection**
- Identifies students with wait times >21 days
- Highlights potential academic disruptions
- Supports proactive student advising interventions
- Measures institutional responsiveness to enrollment challenges

**✅ Queue Justice Metrics**
- Monitors wait time equality across student demographics
- Identifies systematic barriers in course access
- Supports diversity, equity, and inclusion initiatives
- Measures effectiveness of academic advising programs

### Departmental Insights

**✅ Cross-Enrollment Pattern Analysis**
- Reveals course sequence bottlenecks
- Identifies prerequisite constraint impacts
- Maps academic pathway efficiencies
- Supports curriculum design optimization

**✅ Peer Comparison Analytics**
- Compares capacity utilization across departments
- Identifies high-demand programs requiring resources
- Reveals trends in academic popularity shifts
- Guides strategic enrollment target setting
